## Legal Notes

### CC0 Summary

CC0 (Creative Commons Zero) enables creators to waive all copyright and related rights in their works to the fullest extent allowed by law. When a creator uses CC0, they are essentially giving up all their copyright and related rights to the work, putting it into the public domain.

**You are free to:**
- Share - copy and redistribute the material in any medium or format
- Adapt - remix, transform, and build upon the material for any purpose, even commercially

**No conditions apply:**
- No attribution required
- No share-alike requirement
- No derivative restrictions

This license documentation is provided for informational purposes. While all effort has been made to ensure accuracy, users should verify license terms at the original source URLs before using these assets in production.
